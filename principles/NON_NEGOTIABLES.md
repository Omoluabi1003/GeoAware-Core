# Non-Negotiables

These rules protect GeoAware's identity.

1. Maps are not decoration. Maps are the product.
2. Geography is the interface.
3. Simplicity beats cleverness.
4. Every feature must deepen exploration, geography, Scripture, journey, language, worship, or guidance.
5. AI supports exploration. AI never replaces the product experience.
6. WaveAtlas is the canonical audio and radio engine for the GeoAware ecosystem.
7. GeoAware Bible is a Geographic Bible Experience, not a generic Bible reader.
8. Bundle non-blocking reviewer nits into the next meaningful PR.
9. Avoid duplicate datasets, duplicate logic, and temporary architecture.
10. Every PR must protect the long-term product identity.
