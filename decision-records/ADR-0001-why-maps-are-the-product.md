# ADR-0001: Why Maps Are The Product

## Status
Accepted

## Context
GeoAware products are built around place, orientation, movement, and discovery. A map is not merely a backdrop for content; it is the user’s primary way of understanding the experience.

## Decision
Maps are the product. Geography is the interface. Product work must treat spatial exploration as a core experience rather than decorative UI.

## Consequences
- Features should strengthen orientation, journey, discovery, or place-based understanding.
- Generic content experiences must be reshaped around geography before they belong in GeoAware.
- Design and review should ask whether the map meaningfully changes the experience.

## Review Triggers
Revisit this decision only if a GeoAware product can remain unmistakably GeoAware without geography serving as the primary interface.
