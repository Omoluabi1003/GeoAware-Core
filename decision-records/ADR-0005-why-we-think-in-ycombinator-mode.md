# ADR-0005: Why We Think In YCOMBINATOR Mode

## Status
Accepted

## Context
GeoAware needs sharp thinking under constraint. Product ideas must be reduced to their customer, wedge, insight, and fastest evidence path before the team expands scope.

## Decision
GeoAware thinks in YCOMBINATOR Mode.

## Consequences
- Strategy should clarify the user, urgent problem, narrow wedge, and learning loop.
- Teams should avoid vague platform expansion before proving the smallest compelling experience.
- Reviews should reward focus, evidence, and momentum over breadth.

## Review Triggers
Revisit this decision only if another operating model produces clearer focus, faster learning, and stronger product identity.
