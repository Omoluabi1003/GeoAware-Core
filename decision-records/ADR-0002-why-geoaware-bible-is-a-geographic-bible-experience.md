# ADR-0002: Why GeoAware Bible Is A Geographic Bible Experience

## Status
Accepted

## Context
A generic Bible reader can present text, search, notes, and reading plans. GeoAware Bible must do something more specific: reveal Scripture through place, journey, language, and historical geography.

## Decision
GeoAware Bible is a Geographic Bible Experience, not a generic Bible reader.

## Consequences
- Scripture features must deepen geographic, historical, linguistic, devotional, or journey-based understanding.
- Commodity Bible-reader capabilities are secondary unless they reinforce the geographic experience.
- AI may assist study and navigation, but it must not displace Scripture, map, journey, or worship as the center of the product.

## Review Triggers
Revisit this decision if the product’s primary user value is no longer Scripture experienced through geography and journey.
