# Design Review

Design review protects clarity, geography, and product identity.

## Review questions

- Is geography central to the experience?
- Does the design invite exploration?
- Is the interface calm and understandable?
- Are maps treated as the product surface, not decoration?
- Is AI used only where it supports exploration?

## Blocking issues

Block changes that make the product generic, confusing, inaccessible, or less geographic.
