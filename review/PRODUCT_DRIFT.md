# Product Drift

Product drift happens when GeoAware begins to behave like a generic app.

## Drift signals

- Decorative maps.
- Feed-first interfaces.
- AI replacing exploration.
- Duplicate data models.
- Features that do not deepen geography, journey, Scripture, language, worship, audio, or guidance.

## Response

Name the drift clearly, reduce scope, and realign the work with the constitution.
