# Product Philosophy

GeoAware products are geographic experiences first.

## Core belief

Place changes meaning. A map is not an accessory to the experience; it is the surface where meaning, movement, and discovery become understandable.

## Experience qualities

GeoAware products should feel:

- Exploratory.
- Calm.
- Contextual.
- Trustworthy.
- Purposeful.
- Lightweight.

## AI philosophy

AI should guide, clarify, translate, summarize, and recommend only when it deepens exploration. AI must not replace the map, the journey, the Scripture, the audio, the language, or the user's own discovery.

## Product identity rule

If a feature could fit unchanged inside a generic consumer app, it needs stronger GeoAware identity before it ships.
