# YCOMBINATOR Mode

YCOMBINATOR Mode is the urgency layer of GeoAware execution.

## Principles

- Build only what teaches or unlocks progress.
- Prefer direct user value over internal polish.
- Make decisions with incomplete information when delay is more expensive than being wrong.
- Keep the roadmap honest.
- Do not confuse activity with progress.

## Questions

- What is the sharpest problem?
- What can ship now?
- What would make this useful this week?
- What can be removed?
- What would a serious founder refuse to overcomplicate?
