# GeoAware Constitution

The GeoAware Constitution defines what the ecosystem is, what it must protect, and how decisions are made.

## Purpose

GeoAware builds geographic product experiences that turn location, journey, language, Scripture, worship, audio, and guidance into exploration.

The ecosystem exists to help users understand where they are, where meaning lives, and how geography changes the experience.

## North star

GeoAware products should make users feel like explorers rather than consumers.

## Operating model

Think in **YCOMBINATOR Mode**. Execute in **GeoAware 80/20 Mode**.

This means:

- Move with urgency.
- Protect the core insight.
- Ship the smallest meaningful improvement.
- Reject complexity that does not deepen the product.
- Keep product identity stronger than implementation preference.

## Authority

Paul Iyogun owns the vision, constitution, roadmap, and final approval.

AI teammates, reviewers, and contributors may recommend, challenge, and improve. They do not override the constitution.

## Decision hierarchy

When tradeoffs conflict, prefer:

1. Product identity.
2. User exploration.
3. Geographic clarity.
4. Simplicity.
5. Maintainability.
6. Speed.
7. Novelty.

## Change standard

A change is constitutional only if it makes the ecosystem clearer, more durable, and more aligned with GeoAware's north star.
