# GeoAware 80/20 Mode

GeoAware 80/20 Mode is the product discipline layer of execution.

## Principles

- Simplicity beats cleverness.
- Maps are not decoration. Maps are the product.
- Geography is the interface.
- Every feature must deepen exploration, geography, Scripture, journey, language, worship, or guidance.
- AI supports exploration. AI never replaces the product experience.

## What to cut

Cut anything that creates duplicate datasets, duplicate logic, temporary architecture, or generic product behavior.

## What to keep

Keep the smallest coherent version of the feature that strengthens GeoAware's long-term identity.
