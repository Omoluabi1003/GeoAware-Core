# QA

QA protects user trust, product identity, and release quality.

## Responsibilities

- Verify acceptance criteria.
- Check regressions.
- Review accessibility, performance, and security implications.
- Confirm that product behavior matches GeoAware doctrine.
- Identify blocking issues before merge.

## Constraints

QA should distinguish blockers from nits. Non-blocking nits should be bundled into the next meaningful PR.
